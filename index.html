Why I Started This

I've always been that person who goes down rabbit holes at 3 AM trying to understand why something works the way it does. You know that feeling when you notice something weird about the world and it just won't leave your brain alone? That's me, constantly.

I started Rogue Knowledge because I realized I wasn't the only one with this obsessive curiosity about random stuff. There are questions out there that deserve answers, trends that need explaining, and internet phenomena that are just begging to be understood.

Fun fact: The name "Rogue Knowledge" isn't just about going off the beaten path - it's actually based on my initials, R.K. Sometimes the best ideas are hiding right in front of you! But honestly, I might explore anything that catches my curiosity, so who knows where this channel will take us.
